This season's challenge centers on game piece scoring. Teams design and build robots capable of collecting and scoring 
game pieces into designated alliance goals. Two alliances, each consisting of three robots, compete head-to-head. The 
objective is to collect game pieces from the field and score them into alliance goals. Game pieces are distributed 
across the field, and robots may collect, control, and score them in designated scoring zones. Each alliance has 
multiple scoring locations with different point values. The alliance with the highest point total at the end of the 
match wins.
